Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Find answers to common questions about how BraveCall works, its features, and how it can help you.

What is BraveCall?

BraveCall is an intelligent system that uses AI to detect gender-based violence (GBV) and provide support services for victims.

How does BraveCall work?

It analyzes reports using AI to detect possible GBV cases and provides immediate assistance by connecting users with relevant support organizations.

What kind of support does BraveCall offer?

It provides emergency assistance, connects users to shelters, legal aid, counseling, and offers AI-driven guidance for immediate help.

Who can use BraveCall?

Anyone who has witnessed or experienced GBV can use BraveCall to report incidents or seek help.

How can incidents be reported?

Users can report incidents through the BraveCall website using text, voice, or multimedia. The system will analyze the report and determine the appropriate response.

Is BraveCall free to use?

Yes, BraveCall offers free essential services for those in need. Some advanced features for organizations may require a subscription.
